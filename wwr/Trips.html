Half-Day Rafting Adventure (Greece)

Difficulty: Beginner to Intermediate

Duration: 4 hours

Price: $75 per person

Experience the thrill of whitewater rafting on a half-day trip perfect for beginners and families. This 4-hour excursion includes professional guides, safety equipment, and stunning views of the river. Navigate Class II rapids while learning about local wildlife and geology. Lunch is provided, and transportation from the meeting point is included.

Full-Day Extreme Rafting

Difficulty: Advanced

Duration: 8 hours

Price: $150 per person

Challenge yourself on our full-day extreme rafting trip, featuring Class III and IV rapids for the adventurous spirit. Led by experienced guides like Ben, this 8-hour journey includes intense paddling, team-building exercises, and breathtaking scenery. Equipment and meals are provided, with options for photography services.

Family Rafting Package

Difficulty: Beginner

Duration: 6 hours

Price: $100 per person

Join our family-friendly rafting package designed for all ages, with gentle Class I and II rapids and expert instruction. This 6-hour trip emphasizes fun and safety, including stops for swimming and picnicking. Our guides, including Joseph, ensure a memorable experience for kids and adults alike. Includes all gear and a BBQ lunch.
